BADKAYNE BIOGRAPHY

Zarek goes by Bad Kayne and if you haven’t heard the name yet, you will.

Born in 2008, he’s not pretending to be a veteran. He’s young, still learning, still experimenting, still figuring things out and that’s exactly where the energy comes from. There’s hunger in the sound. Curiosity. Late nights spent testing ideas, deleting beats, starting again.

He’s not polished by years in the game. He’s driven by obsession.

Right now he’s locked in the studio, building his foundation brick by brick. Trying new textures. Studying drums. Chasing better mixes. Every track is a step forward. Every mistake is part of the process.

No fake image.

No forced hype.

Just a young producer putting in the hours and getting better every single session.
